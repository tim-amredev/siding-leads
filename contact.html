Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical siding installation take?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

Most residential siding projects take between 1-2 weeks to complete, depending on the size of your home, the type of siding being installed, and weather conditions. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life while ensuring quality installation.

Do you offer financing options?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

Yes, we offer several financing options to help make your siding project more affordable. These include 0% interest for 12 months for qualified buyers, low monthly payment plans, and no prepayment penalties. Our team can help you find the right financing solution for your budget.

What warranties do you offer on siding installation?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

We provide a 5-year workmanship warranty on all our siding installations. Additionally, the siding materials themselves come with manufacturer warranties that range from 25 years to lifetime limited warranties, depending on the product you choose. We'll provide detailed warranty information for your specific siding selection.

Do I need to be home during the siding installation?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

While you don't need to be present for the entire installation process, we do recommend being available at the beginning of the project to discuss any final details with our team, and at the end for a final walkthrough and inspection. Our project manager will coordinate with you to determine the best schedule.
