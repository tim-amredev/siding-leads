Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the online estimate?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

Our online estimator provides a ballpark figure based on average costs in your area and the information you provide. While it's a good starting point, the final cost may vary based on factors that can only be assessed during an in-home consultation, such as the condition of your existing structure, accessibility issues, or custom design elements.

What factors might cause my actual quote to differ from the online estimate?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

Several factors can affect your final quote, including: the exact measurements of your home, the condition of your existing siding and underlying structure, any necessary repairs, complex architectural features, accessibility challenges, custom color or design choices, and local building code requirements. Our in-home consultation will address all these factors for an accurate quote.

Do I need to be present for the in-home consultation?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

Yes, we recommend that you be present during the in-home consultation. This allows our siding specialist to discuss your preferences, answer your questions in real-time, and address any specific concerns you might have about your project. It also gives you the opportunity to see samples of different siding materials and colors in your home's natural lighting.

How long does the estimate process take?

{% include icons/plus.html %}

The online estimate takes just a few minutes to complete. For the in-home consultation, you should allow approximately 60-90 minutes. During this time, our specialist will measure your home, assess your current siding, discuss your preferences, and provide you with a detailed quote. We value your time and strive to be thorough yet efficient.
